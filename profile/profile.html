McDonald's?

McDonald's is a global fast food chain with a significant presence in many countries around the world. As a large corporation, it has a formal vision and mission statement that guides its operations and business decisions. The following is McDonald's vision and mission statements:

Vision:

"To be the world's best quick-service restaurant experience. Being the best means providing outstanding quality, service, cleanliness, and value, so that we make every customer in every restaurant smile. "

Mission:

"Our mission is to be our customers' favorite place and way to eat and drink. We are committed to being America's best first job. Through the hard work and dedication of our local franchisees, employees, and suppliers, we are committed to serving our customers and communities with integrity, innovation, and a passion for being the best."
